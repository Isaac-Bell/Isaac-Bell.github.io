Core Reflections

What are three new things you have learned about yourself and your ego as a result of the core learning?

Some of the key things that stood out to me was the neuroplacity topic. Sometimes in my life i've thought i'm past my learning date (since i'm not a kid anymore it will be hard for me to learn.) This content really reinforces the idea that if i want to learn something enough, I can!

What are the role of values, empathy and self awareness in learning and programming?

Similar to what i mentioned above i think, is the value of kindness. Kindess to others and self allows space to learn. When I don't know something I don't get upset if i'm kind to myself, I just say I need to give myelf a chance to learn these things, I just need to give my brain more time to come to terms with this. I think self awareness of my inner critic can be good too, in terms of my effort. Am i putting enough effort in? Can I do more? Often the two can go hand in hand to birth joyful productivity.

What has surprised you the most about the core learning?

To be honest, alot of the content I've already read books about, and was one of the things that attracted my friend to the enspiral group, which is how we are good friends. I love the style of learning that EDA presents.

What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

To be honest, probably the relfections. Although the content wasn't new to me, i havn't been required to talk about it before like this, and in that sense thinking and refelcting on how the conent impacts has been good for me but challenging.
